# Company Vacation Policy

Employees get 20 days of paid vacation per year.
Managers get 25 days of paid vacation per year.
Vacation requests must be approved by your manager at least 2 weeks in advance.
Unused vacation days expire at the end of the year.

# Remote Work Policy

All employees can work remotely on Mondays and Fridays.
Managers must be in office Tuesday-Thursday.
Software engineers have flexible hours between 10 AM - 4 PM core hours.
Sales team must attend all client meetings in person.

# Salary Information (Confidential - Managers Only)

Entry-level engineers: $80,000 - $100,000
Senior engineers: $120,000 - $150,000
Managers: $140,000 - $180,000
Bonuses are 10-20% based on performance.
